## Exercise 1: Generating a Short Story from a Theme

### What to Do
1. Choose a theme (friendship, space exploration, overcoming fear)
2. Write a simple prompt: "Write a short story about [theme] in under 300 words"
3. Test with at least 2 different themes

### Example Themes

**Theme: Friendship**
> "Write a short story about friendship in under 300 words."

**Output:**
> Maya sat alone at the coffee shop. Sarah asked if she could sit. They talked for hours. That random meeting became a lifelong friendship. Sometimes the best friends are strangers who become family.

**Theme: Space Exploration**
> "Write a short story about space exploration in under 300 words."

### Key Takeaway
The same prompt structure produces different stories based on your theme. Be specific about your theme!

## Exercise 2: Personalized Resume Summary

### What to Do
Create prompts that generate resume summaries for different roles.

### Template
> "Create a 3-sentence professional summary for a [ROLE] skilled in [SKILLS]."

### Examples

**Software Engineer:**
> "Create a 3-sentence professional summary for a software engineer skilled in Python and cloud systems."

**Output:**
> Results-driven software engineer with 5+ years experience in Python and cloud technologies. Built scalable microservices architecture and led DevOps initiatives. Passionate about clean code and mentoring junior developers.

**Teacher:**
> "Create a 3-sentence professional summary for a teacher skilled in curriculum development and classroom management."

### Key Takeaway
Just change the role and skills to get a tailored summary!

## Exercise 3: Image Generation Prompt Design

### What to Do
Create prompts for image generation models (DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, etc.)

### âš ï¸ Important: Avoid Generic AI Look

**BAD (Too Common):**
- "A futuristic city"
- "A beautiful sunset"
- "A cyberpunk street"

**GOOD (Personal/Unique):**
- "My grandmother's kitchen in Lagos"
- "The busy market in my hometown"
- "My childhood bedroom with posters on the wall"

### Vague vs Refined

**Vague:**
> "A city"

**Refined:**
> "A busy Nigerian street market at morning, with traders selling fruits and vegetables, golden sunrise lighting, realistic photograph"

### Refined (Personal - Nigeria)

**Prompt:**
> "A busy Nigerian street market at morning, with traders selling fruits and vegetables, golden sunrise lighting, warm colors, realistic photograph"

![Nigerian Market](ex3-nigerian-market.png)

1. **Be Specific:** Add details about location, time, mood
2. **Include Medium:** "watercolor," "oil painting," "photograph"
3. **Add Personal Touch:** Your own memories, hometown, culture
4. **Set the Scene:** "at morning market," "during rainy season"
